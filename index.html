Teaching Tools

Making Choices in Free Market Systems — Mouli Modak

Interactive explainers and classroom tools for BA 1310. Each tool runs entirely in the browser — no login, no install. Students can open them on laptops or phones.

Available tools

Financial statements walkthrough

Post transactions one at a time and watch them flow through the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Covers revenue, COGS, gross profit, EBIT, EBT, tax, and net income. Five different business scenarios to choose from.

Interactive Accounting 5 scenarios

Ratio analysis

From financial statement, calculate different ratios used to make financial decision. Covers an example of choosing one company to acquire using these ratio, and 4 examples of using transactions to create the financial statements and then the ratios.

Interactive Accounting 1 + 4 scenarios

Value of Time

This contains practice problems to calculate future value or present value given interest rates and time.

Interactive Economics Practice Problems

Decision under Uncertainty

This contains practice problems for calculating expected value and explanation for two kinds of paradoxes about decision under uncertainty.
