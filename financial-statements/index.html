Reading the income statement: Gross profit = Revenue − COGS (what's left after making the product). EBIT (Earnings Before Interest and Taxes, also called operating income) = Gross profit − operating expenses. EBT (Earnings Before Taxes) = EBIT − interest. Net income = EBT − taxes — what actually flows to the owner.
